What are AP Courses?

Advanced Placement (AP) courses are college-level classes offered by the College Board. Students can take AP exams in May, and scoring 3 or higher can earn college credit at universities worldwide. AP exams are scored on a 1-5 scale, with 5 being the highest.

AP courses demonstrate academic rigor and are highly valued by admissions officers at US universities. They allow students to explore subjects at a deeper level while still in high school.
